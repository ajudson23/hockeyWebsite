A little about us ...

The Colorado Springs Adult Hockey league is for players that are 18 years and older.
This league is open to female and male players.
The season runs annually where typically there will be 28-32 games in a season take a week off & then play another 38-32 games.

Advanced League

The advanced league is for players that have typically played at the high school or higher levels.
The advanced league plays on wednesdays at Ed Ropson Arena with game times at 6pm, 7:15pm, 8:30pm, & 9:45pm

Intermediate league

The intermediate league is generally older & less experienced then the advanced players
The intermediate league plays on mondays with games 6pm, 7:15pm, 8:30pm, & 9:45pm
